9.1.1 Feature types

A. CDM has stricter requirements for mandatory coordinates (Table 9.1)

featureType

CF mandatory coordinates

CDM Mandatory coordinates

point

at least one coordinate

lat, lon, t

timeSeries

t coordinate

lat, lon, t

trajectory

at least two spatial coordinates

lat, lon, t

profile

z coordinate

lat, lon, z, t

timeSeriesProfile

z and t coordinates

lat, lon, z, t

trajectoryProfile

x, y and z coordinates

lat, lon, z, t

B. horizontal coordinates

  • CF: "spatial coordinates x and y usually refer to longitude and latitude but other horizontal coordinates could also be used"
  • CDM: only latitude and longitude are allowed.

C. vertical coordinates

  • CDM: vertical coordinate may be height or pressure. Dimensionless Vertical Coordinates are not supported.

9.1.3 Representations

A. Dimension ordering

  • CF: "the data variable has the instance and element dimensions, which may be in any order".
  • CDM: the instance dimension must be the outer (slowest varying) dimension

9.1.4 featureType attribute

A. Optional attribute

  • CF: not required
  • CDM: The global attribute featureType is required.
